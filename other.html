Overview

The books featured on this page are stand alone books that have been written by Brandon Sanderson, currently they are all single books, however Brandon has suggested that he may expand on these books with sequals and create proper series out of them.

Elantris

Discover where the magic began...

Elantris was a place of glory

The capital of Arelon, the home to people transformed into magic-using demigods by the Shaod.

But then the magic failed, Elantris started to rot, and its inhabitants turned into powerless wrecks.

And in the new capital, Kae, close enough to Elantris for everyone to be reminded of what they have lost, a princess arrives. Sarene is to be married to unite Teod and Arelon against the religious imperialists of Fjordell. But she is told that Raoden, her husband to be, is dead.

Determined to carry on the fight for Teod and Arelon's freedom, Sarene clashes with the high priest Hrathen. If hrathen can persuade the populace to convert, Fjordell will reign supreme.

But there are secrets in Elantris, the dead and the ruined may yet have a role to play in this new world. Magic Lives.

Warbreaker

Magic as you have never seen it before.

Warbreaker is the story of two sisters, who happen to be princesses, the God King one of them has to marry, a lesser god, and an immortal trying to undo the mistakes he made hundreds of years ago.

Theirs is a world in which those who die in glory return as gods to live confined to a pantheon in Hallandren's capital city. A world transfromed by BioChromatic magic, a power based on an essence known as breath. Using magic is arduous: breath can only be collected one unit at a time from individual people.

But the rewards are great: by using breath and drawing upon the colour in everyday objest, all manner of miracles and mischief can be performed.

Brandon Sanderson proves again that he is a master of what Tolkien called "secondary creation," the invention of whole worlds, complete with magics and myths all their own.

Arcanum Unbounded: The Cosmere Collection

Arcnaum unbounded is a series of short stories that are all related to all of the major books Brandon has written, these are stories that are either set on the same world as one of the stories, or stories that involve major or minor characters from the main books.

the stories that are included in Arcanum Unbounded are:

  • The emperors Soul
  • The Hope of Elantris
  • The Elevnth Metal
  • Allomancer Jak and the Pits of Eltania
  • Mistborn: Secret History
  • White Sand
  • Shadows for Silence in the Forests of Hell
  • Sixth of the Dusk
  • Edgedancer

This collection of nie works, originally published individually, have been collected for the first time and convey the true expanse of the Cosmere. Telling the exciting tales Sanderson fans have come to expect, Arcanum Unbounded includes the hugo Award-wining novella "The Emperor's Soul", an excerpt from the graphic novel "White Sand", and the never-before-pulbished Stormlight Archive novella "Edgedancer."

This superb collection also includes essays and illistrations which offer an insight into the numerous worlds in which the stories are set.
